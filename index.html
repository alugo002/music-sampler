RUN THE JEWELS

Run The Jewels is a rap duo composed of hip-hop veterans Killer Mike and El-P. Killer Mike was introduced to the hip-hop scene through his cousin Big Boi, who is one half of the more popular rap duo from the south called Outkast. El-P got his start in Brooklyn's alternative hip-hop scene. Both have been around for decades, but had yet to make a name for themselves until they united in the 2010s. After a long struggle trying to make it onto the scene, both are enjoying a great amount of success as they've sold out venues in the U.S. and Europe, gotten critical acclaim and host legends on their albums such as Rage Against the Machine's Zack de la Rocha, 2 Chainz. They both come from completely different spectrums of the hip-hop scene, which is what makes them sound so unique. El-P serves as the duo's producer. His bizarre and futuristic beats have proven to be a great backdrop for Killer Mike's pugilistic verses and El-P's cynical but sometimes comical lyrics. Although their lyrics focus on their similarly dark outlooks on society, I tend to look at it as a story of two best friends living through a dystopia together.
